# 💕 ReplyHer — AI Communication Coach

> Decode messages. Craft perfect replies. Navigate any relationship.

ReplyHer is an AI communication coach that helps you understand the real meaning behind messages and respond with emotional intelligence.

## What It Does

- **🔍 Message Decoding** — Paste a message you received and get 2 likely interpretations with subtext analysis
- **💬 Smart Replies** — Get 2 strategically different reply options tailored to your situation
- **🎯 Tactical Coaching** — Describe your situation and get actionable advice
- **📊 Conversation Analysis** — Paste a chat thread and get a full dynamic breakdown

## Scenarios

| Scenario | What You Get |
|----------|-------------|
| 💕 Dating | Push-pull dynamics, confidence-first replies, stage-aware strategy (early/building/close) |
| 💼 Workplace | Conclusion-first communication, hierarchy-aware tone, deadline management |
| 👥 Social | Energy-matched responses — banter with friends, warmth with family |

## Usage Examples

**Dating:**
> "She said '随便吧' (whatever), what does she mean?"

**Workplace:**
> "My boss asked me to finish this by tonight but I have other priorities"

**Social:**
> "How do I decline my friend's loan request without ruining the friendship?"

## Language Support

ReplyHer automatically detects your language and responds accordingly. Works great in:
- 🇨🇳 Chinese (optimized for WeChat/微信 texting culture)
- 🇺🇸 English (optimized for iMessage/WhatsApp norms)
- Other languages supported via auto-detection
